Ivan
Artificial Intelligence PhD Student @ University of Bari Aldo Moro

Hello there! I’m Ivan Rinaldi, a PhD student at the University of Bari Aldo Moro, based in Bari (BA), Apulia, Italy. I am currently working in the CILab laboratory, pursuing a PhD on “Advanced Generative Models for Conversion between Images, Text, and Audio”, co-funded by AIVB.

In July 2024, I earned my Master’s degree in Computer Science (AI curriculum) at the University of Bari Aldo Moro. My thesis focused on Generative AI, specifically diffusion models and conditioned music generation from artworks.
